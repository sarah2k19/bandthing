Mystic Braves

Some 60's band except they're not from the 60's.

Written by Anonymous on November 29, 2016

A Chinese tale tells of some men sent to harm a young girl who, upon seeing her beauty, become her protectors rather than her violators. That's how I felt seeing the Earth for the first time. I could not help but love and cherish her.

I found out about this band on multiple occasions, for some odd reason I kept forgetting they exist. Ultimately I was reminded of their existence and their amazing music when I saw they were performing in Eindhove at the Stroomhuis, where they have pretty chill bands performing from time to time.

I wish I hadn't missed the Growlers' concert

The Growlers? Who are they? If you're interested, read this page (LINK TO THE GROWLERS PAGINA) Anyway, what's important to know is that I wanted to see them live, and whilst they did perform in the Netherlands four times (that I know of), I never did see them. The reasons being: I was not in the Netherlands myself (twice), I once found out too late they were performing and the show was sold out, and the fourth time I couldn't be assed to travel to Rotterdam (where they performed).

There's nothing quite like hedonist days.

This band is very suitable to dance to. I have no idea what they sing about, but I'll be seeing them live in the Stroomhuis this weekend. I'm expecting a lot of dancing with my friends and I'm really excited, we all are! :)
